Detailed Description

Top level PseudoTcp object, which contains TX and TX parts.

Has two primary purposes:

  1. Act as encapsulator and facade for PseudoTcpTx and PseudoTcpRx
  2. Handle incoming messages, sending them to RX (if payload) or TX (if ack info) as needed.
