MyFSA is a social network aiming to connect the companies of Sophia-Antipolis. It is based on myMed.

myMed was created to help the development of economic activities in the French/Italian border area, through the action Alcotra.

The proposal myMed is a collaboration of more than 15 years between the INRIA in Sophia Antipolis and the Polytechnic of Turin, plus two other partners, the University of Turin and the University of Eastern Piedmont.
